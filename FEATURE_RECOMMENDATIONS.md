## 🔥 Priority 1: Essential Missing Features

### 1. **Prescription Management System**
**Why:** Core functionality for any medical practice
- Digital prescription creation and printing
- Template management (common medications/dosages)
- Prescription history per patient
- Medication interaction warnings
- Prescription renewal tracking
- E-prescription capability (if regulations allow)
- Pharmacy integration suggestions

### 2. **SMS/Email Notifications & Reminders**
**Why:** Critical for patient engagement and reducing no-shows
- Automated appointment reminders (24h before, 1h before)
- Appointment confirmation SMS/Email
- Prescription ready notifications
- Lab result notifications
- Follow-up reminders
- Birthday wishes with health tips
- Health tips/educational content distribution

### 3. **Telemedicine/Video Consultation**
**Why:** Modern healthcare necessity, especially post-COVID
- Integrated video consultation scheduling
- Video call functionality (WebRTC)
- Consultation recording (with consent)
- Remote prescription during video calls
- Virtual waiting room
- Screen sharing for reports/images

### 4. **Billing & Payment System**
**Why:** Essential for practice revenue management
- Invoice generation
- Payment tracking (cash, card, mobile banking)
- Payment history per patient
- Outstanding balance tracking
- Payment reminders
- Receipt generation
- Financial reports (daily, monthly, yearly)
- Integration with popular payment gateways (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)

### 5. **Medical Reports & Documents Management**
**Why:** Better organization and quick access
- Upload/download medical documents (test reports, scans, images)
- Document categorization (Lab Reports, X-Rays, Prescriptions, etc.)
- PDF report generation
- Share reports with patients via secure links
- Document versioning
- Cloud storage integration

### 6. **Analytics & Reporting Dashboard**
**Why:** Data-driven decision making
- Patient demographics analysis
- Disease/condition statistics
- Peak appointment hours analysis
- Revenue analytics
- Appointment no-show rate
- Patient retention metrics
- Treatment success tracking
- Custom report builder
