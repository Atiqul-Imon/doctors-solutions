## 🚀 Quick Start Steps

1. **Week 1**: Setup + Launch Awareness ads
2. **Week 2**: Launch Consideration ads, start retargeting
3. **Week 3**: Launch Conversion ads, scale winners

---

## 💡 Top Tips

1. Start small, scale gradually
2. Test 3-5 ad variations always
3. Respond to messages within 1 hour
4. Focus on quality leads over quantity
5. Use Bengali for all ads
6. Show value, not just features
7. Use social proof (testimonials, numbers)
8. Create urgency (limited offers)
9. Track everything (UTM, Pixel)
10. Be patient, optimize continuously
