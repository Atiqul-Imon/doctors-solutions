## 🔄 FUNNEL STAGES

### Stage 1: AWARENESS (Top of Funnel)
**Objective**: Reach maximum doctors, create awareness
**Budget**: 40% of total budget

### Stage 2: CONSIDERATION (Middle of Funnel)
**Objective**: Engage interested doctors, nurture leads
**Budget**: 35% of total budget

### Stage 3: CONVERSION (Bottom of Funnel)
**Objective**: Convert leads into paying clients
**Budget**: 25% of total budget

## 🎯 TRACKING & OPTIMIZATION

### Key Metrics to Track

**Awareness Stage:**
- Reach
- Impressions
- Link Clicks
- Cost per Click (CPC)
- Page Likes

**Consideration Stage:**
- Lead Form Completions
- Cost per Lead (CPL)
- Video Views (75%+)
- Engagement Rate
- Message Responses

**Conversion Stage:**
- Conversions (Consultation Bookings)
- Cost per Conversion
- Messenger Conversations
- Sales/Revenue
- ROI

### Tools Needed

1. **Facebook Pixel** (Must have!)
   - Track website visitors
   - Conversion tracking
   - Retargeting data

2. **Google Analytics**
   - Track user behavior
   - Conversion funnels
   - Source attribution

3. **UTM Parameters**
   - Track ad performance
   - Identify best ads
   - Optimize budget

### Optimization Rules

**Pause Ads If:**
- CPC > ৳৫০ (after 3 days)
- CPL > ৳৫০০ (after 3 days)
- No conversions after ৳৩,০০০ spent

**Scale Ads If:**
- CPC < ৳২০
- CPL < ৳৩০০
- Conversion rate > 2%
- Positive ROI

**Test Continuously:**
- New ad creatives every week
- Different headlines
- Different CTAs
- Different audiences

## 🎨 AD CREATIVE GUIDELINES

### Image Specifications
- **Facebook Feed**: 1200 x 628px
- **Instagram Feed**: 1080 x 1080px (Square) or 1080 x 1350px (Portrait)
- **Stories**: 1080 x 1920px
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **File size**: < 30MB

### Video Specifications
- **Duration**: 15-60 seconds (optimal: 30 seconds)
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 1:1
- **Resolution**: 1080p minimum
- **Format**: MP4, MOV
- **Sound**: Optional (but recommended)
- **Captions**: Required (many watch without sound)

### Design Principles
1. **Clear Value Proposition**: What do they get?
2. **Professional Look**: Medical/Healthcare aesthetic
3. **Bengali Text**: Clear, readable fonts
4. **Strong CTA**: Action-oriented button text
5. **Trust Elements**: Logos, testimonials, numbers

### Color Scheme (Match Brand)
- Primary: Blue/Teal (Medical trust)
- Secondary: White (Clean, professional)
- Accent: Green (Health, growth)
