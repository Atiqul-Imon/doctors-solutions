## 🎯 Design Principles Applied

1. **Authenticity over Marketing**
   - Real information only
   - No fabricated metrics
   - Verifiable credentials

2. **Personal over Institutional**
   - First-person voice ("আমি")
   - Individual doctor brand
   - Personal story and philosophy

3. **Clarity over Complexity**
   - One clear CTA in hero
   - Dedicated info sections
   - Simple language

4. **Credibility over Claims**
   - BMDC registration shown
   - Hospital affiliation clear
   - Specific visiting hours
   - Real contact information

5. **Human over Corporate**
   - Warm, respectful tone
   - Treatment philosophy shared
   - Personal commitment stated
   - Cultural sensitivity (Friday closed)
